Knife Skills

Proper knife skills are essential for safe and efficient cooking. Learn the basics of holding a knife and chopping vegetables.

Measuring Ingredients

Accurate measuring ensures your recipes turn out right. Use cups, spoons, and scales appropriately.

  • Dry Ingredients: Use measuring cups and level off with a knife.
  • Liquid Ingredients: Use a liquid measuring cup and check at eye level.
  • Small Quantities: Use measuring spoons for spices and small amounts.

Understanding Cooking Terms

Sauté
Cook quickly in a small amount of oil.
Bake
Cook in an oven with dry heat.
Simmer
Cook gently just below boiling point.
Boil
Cook in boiling water or broth.
